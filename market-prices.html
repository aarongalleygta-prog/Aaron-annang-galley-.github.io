Market Tips

  • Prices are 15-20% higher in urban markets (Accra, Kumasi)
  • Wednesday and Saturday markets have better prices
  • Group selling through cooperatives gives better bargaining power
  • Grading and sorting can increase prices by 10-30%
  • Transportation costs: GHS 15-20 per bag from farm to market

Data Source: Ministry of Food and Agriculture (MoFA)
